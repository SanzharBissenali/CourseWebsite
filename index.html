Chemistry Course

Course Description

An introduction to general chemistry concepts with a focus on structure and bonding. Concepts will be tied to fundamental principles related to energy sustainability. Descriptions of atoms, both the physical and electronic structure with an introduction to quantum mechanics; chemical bonding models building up from molecules to extended solids; periodic trends; electrochemistry; and descriptions of states of matter.

Course Textbook

The course textbook is Cambridge International AS and A level Chemistry Coursebookby Roger Norris, Lawrie Ryan, and David Acaster. The book will assist you immensely throughout the course and give you essential knowledge if you study chemistry in depth at university. You can find it in the library, but make sure to borrow it earlier since there are not enough books for everyone. You may also use an electronic version.
